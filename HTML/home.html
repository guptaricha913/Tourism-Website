Gujarat(A Glorious Indian state)

Gujarat is a state in the western part of India known locally as Jewel of the West. It has an area of 196,204 km2 with a coastline of 1,600 km, most of which lies on the Kathiawar peninsula, and a population in excess of 60 million.
