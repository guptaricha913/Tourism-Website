GANDHI CIRCUIT– 3NIGHTS / 4DAYS
Day 1
 Arrive at Porbandar where Gandhiji was born on 2nd October 1869 in a prominent family. His house in the old walled quarters of the city where Gandhiji was born is now open to visitors. Beside the haveli is the Kirti Mandir built in memory of the Mahatma Gandhi which has a museum, picture gallery and library. Evening meditation at Porbandar, dinner and night halt.
0 kms
Day 2
 After breakfast, proceed to Rajkot where Gandhiji went to school. See his childhood home called Kaba Gandhi no Delo which has a display of artifacts and photographs from the Mahatma’s life. In the evening, visit the Sabarmati Ashram at Ahmedabad from where Mahatma launched his struggle for freedom. Dinner and night halt at Ahmedabad.
360 kms
Day 3
 After breakfast, go for the Heritage Walk, a 1.5 km walking tour. The walking tour will take us through old quarters and cul-de-sacs called pols to see havelis, courtyard temples with intricate woodwork, textile, silver jewellery, gold, stationery and vegetable market, and the mausoleum of Ahmed Shah who founded Ahmedabad, before ending at Jumma Masjid, a 15th century mosque rated among the most impressive Islamic monuments in India. Post lunch departure for Surat. Dinner & night halt at Surat.
285 kms
Day 4
 Departure to Dandi after early breakfast. Visit Dandi (the destination of Salt Satyagraha). Departure for Ahmedabad via Vadodara (visit Gaekwad Museum and Baroda Museum). Arrival at Ahmedabad by late evening.
400 kms
